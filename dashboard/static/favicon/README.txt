Parlay site icons — add your files here
========================================

1. favicon.ico
   Browsers expect this. Export as 32×32 or 16×16; save as
   "favicon.ico" in this folder. It is served as /favicon.ico and
   /static/favicon/favicon.ico when present.

2. Optional
   - favicon.svg   Replace the placeholder SVG for modern browsers.
   - apple-touch-icon.png  (e.g. 180×180) for iOS home screen.

3. After replacing files, hard-refresh the site (Ctrl+F5) to avoid cache.

The bundled favicon.svg is only a default placeholder; replace with your
branding or rely on favicon.ico only.
